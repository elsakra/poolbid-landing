FAQ

Common questions

Why not go straight to a builder? expand_more
You can—but margin and commissions are often embedded. When you arrive with independent plans and specs, builders know you can compare, which pulls pricing toward a competitive baseline.
Do you build the pool? expand_more
No. We are independent advocates and do not take kickbacks from builders.
What if I already have a sketch? expand_more
We can use it as the starting point for technical plans that support accurate, comparable bids.
How long does the process take? expand_more
Roughly 5–7 days for an initial design pass and about two weeks to first bids—market dependent.
What happens after I submit the form? expand_more
You will see an on-page confirmation and receive an email from our form provider. We reply within one business day with next steps—or a polite decline if we are not a fit.
