Common Questions

Why wouldn't I just go to a builder directly?

You can, but builders include high sales commissions in their quotes. By bringing your own design and specs, you signal you're an informed buyer, forcing them to bid their most competitive 'real' price.

Do you build the pool?

No. We are independent advocates. We never take kickbacks from builders. Our only loyalty is to you and your budget.

What if I already have a sketch?

That's great! We can use your sketch as a starting point to create the technical architectural plans required for accurate bidding.

How long does the process take?

Typically, we have your 3D design finished in 5-7 days and your first round of bids back within 14 days.
