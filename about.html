Independent advocacy for homeowners

What we do

PoolBidder helps homeowners navigate pool design and bidding with clarity. We focus on plans and specifications you can own, bid packages that compare apples to apples, and a managed process that reduces sales pressure—so you can evaluate builders on scope and value, not pitch decks alone.

Independence

We are not a pool builder. We do not take undisclosed kickbacks to steer you toward a particular contractor. Our role is to represent your interests in design alignment and bid readiness.

Where we work

We work with homeowners across the United States. Availability and timelines can vary by market and project scope—we are upfront when we are not the right fit.
